# Propane vs Wood Fire Pits

Choosing between a propane and wood fire pit comes down to how you actually use your backyard. Both can create warmth and atmosphere, but they offer very different day-to-day experiences. One is easier and cleaner. The other feels more traditional and immersive.

## Quick Overview

Propane fire pits are best for convenience, consistent flames, and low maintenance. Wood fire pits are best for people who enjoy the sound, smell, and ritual of a real fire. The right option depends on your space, tolerance for cleanup, and local bylaws.

## Practical Advice

Choose propane if your priority is ease, cleaner operation, or a smaller patio layout. Choose wood if your yard has the space for it and you value the full fire experience enough to handle the extra work. If you live in a windy area or use your backyard casually during the week, propane is usually the more practical fit.
