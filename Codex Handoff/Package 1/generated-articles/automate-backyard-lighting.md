# Automate Backyard Lighting

Automating backyard lighting is one of the easiest ways to make an outdoor space feel more polished and more useful. Instead of remembering switches or timers every evening, you can set lights to match sunset, gatherings, or your usual nighttime routine. The result is better convenience, more consistent curb appeal, and less wasted energy.

## Best Lighting to Automate

Decorative and routine lighting are the best candidates for automation. Path lights with built-in solar charging are already mostly automatic, while plug-in string lights become much more convenient when paired with smart controls.

## Practical Advice

Automate the main seating or dining area first. That gives the biggest payoff because it is the part of the yard people actually use. Keep the schedule simple at first, such as on at dusk and off before bedtime. If the setup works well, expand to other zones later.

For mixed lighting systems, let solar lights handle walkways and let smart plugs manage plug-in decorative lighting. That combination is usually affordable, practical, and easy to maintain.
